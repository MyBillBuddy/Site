Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a bill?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, fill in the bill name, amount, due date, and category, then tap Save.

How do I set up notifications?

When creating or editing a bill, enable the reminder toggle and choose how many days before the due date you'd like to be notified.

How do I mark a bill as paid?

On the home screen, tap the bill and use the "Mark as Paid" button. You can also undo this at any time.

Can I use MyBillBuddy on multiple devices?

Yes — your data is synced to your account so you can access it on any device you're signed in to.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage and cancel your subscription through your App Store account settings at any time.
